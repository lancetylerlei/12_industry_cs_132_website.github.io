And this is our project, Financier—a data-driven investigation into the ratings of financial technology apps in the Philippines. With Google Store reviews as our currency and Python (and its friends) as our broker, we're here to decode the trends behind app ratings.

What motivated this project?

As fintech becomes an essential part of daily life, understanding user feedback is crucial to improving trust, usability, and innovation in local financial services.

By June 2024, mobile fintech had tranformed the financial landscape, with 76.2% of adults—an impressive 63.1 million unique users—embracing digital finance since its rise in September 2018 (Arasa, 2024).

Understanding the factors shaping fintech app ratings in the Philippines, how version updates affect user satisfaction, and the performance of various developers is vital for enhancing digital financial services and ensuring an improved user experience.

This research examines the factors influencing fintech app ratings in the Philippines, the impact of version updates on user satisfaction, and the performance of different apps or developers. The goal is to offer insights that help enhance app quality, user experience, and trust in digital financial services.
